# Marketing Communications

## What We Send

If you opt in, we may send you:

- Course recommendations and updates
- Promotional offers and discounts
- Newsletter with educational content
- Platform feature announcements

## Frequency

We aim to send no more than 1-2 emails per week.

## Opting Out

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by:

- Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email
- Updating your preferences in your account settings

## Data Usage

Your email address is used solely for sending the communications you've opted into. We do not sell or share your email with third parties for their marketing purposes.
